Thesis
Kultoura

The Kultoura project is dedicated to developing an AR-driven progressive web application (PWA) for the SLU Museum of Igorot Culture and Arts. This application aims to enhance the visitor experience by allowing users to access the website and scan QR codes of artifacts within the museum. Upon scanning, detailed information about each artifact is displayed, accompanied by a virtual avatar named Taraki, who provides engaging explanations. By combining user-friendly interfaces with comprehensive data management tools, Kultoura fosters cultural education and accessibility, enriching visitors' understanding of heritage and artifacts.
